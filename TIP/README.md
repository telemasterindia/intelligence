# TIP — TeleMaster Intelligence Platform

Sprint 0 foundation for the TeleMaster Intelligence Platform, an internal local-first lead intelligence platform.

TIP is not a CRM, dialer, lead management SaaS, billing system, or public application. The foundation is designed for one company, one office, and a small internal team while keeping the architecture ready for millions of leads and years of historical intelligence.
