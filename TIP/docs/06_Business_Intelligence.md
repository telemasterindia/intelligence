# Business Intelligence

Business Intelligence turns operational history into financial and strategic understanding.

## Goals

- Compare vendor performance.
- Compare campaign performance.
- Track revenue, conversion, cancellation, and ROI signals.
- Identify profitable and unprofitable segments.
- Support budget and prioritization decisions.

## Business Dimensions

TIP should eventually analyze:

- Vendor.
- Campaign.
- State.
- County.
- ZIP.
- Area code.
- Time window.
- Agent.
- Import batch.
- Lead source age.

## Key Metrics

Potential metrics include:

- Contact rate.
- Qualification rate.
- Sale rate.
- Cancellation rate.
- Revenue per lead.
- Cost per lead.
- Cost per sale.
- Net ROI.
- Validation loss rate.
- Duplicate rate.

## Design Rule

Business Intelligence should be explainable. A manager should be able to trace a metric back to source outcomes, imports, campaigns, and rules.
